Biography

AI and Computer Vision Researcher, PhD candidate
Universidad Rey Juan Carlos (URJC), Madrid // Arquimea Research Center, Madrid and Tenerife

I am an Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision researcher, currently pursuing my Ph.D. in the field of material digitalization and image intrinsic decomposition using neural networks. My research focuses on leveraging advanced techniques such as 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) to analyze and capture materials' visual and mechanical properties in a digital format. I also explore the use of this digitalized materials in physically based simulation using the Material Point Method (MPM) to create realistic animations. Additionally, I also have experience in data analysis and data engineering, enabling me to extract valuable insights and drive impactful outcomes from complex datasets.
